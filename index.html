NEET computers

NEET computers is a Minecraft mod that adds well cleverly implemented computers into the Minecraft world capable of running custom scripts and interacting with the world around them.

The mod includes a emphasis on making computers feel well integrated into the world visualy and mechanically The ingame computers simulate events, lua scripts, peripheral devices, full files systems (with OS's plural that can be edited live), complete Computer Craft compatibility (as of now peripherals kinda work, craft os emulation soon), pixel graphics, HTTPS (soon), and full documentation
